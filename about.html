About Me
 
Danielle Culp
Web Designer, Graphic Designer, Photographer

My name is Danielle Culp and I’m a web design and interactive student at Austin Community College working towards a certificate specializing in web design and interactive technology as well as a full-time quality assistance analyst at HFF. I’ve been in Austin for two years this June and I made the move to Austin because I knew this would be the perfect place to be able to do all of my favorite things (music, art, design, film, outdoors, entertainment, TEXAS) all in one spot and use Austin as a muse for my creativity. I’m originally from Amarillo, Texas and graduated from Texas Tech University with a bachelor’s degree in advertising. I’ve also found a passion in photography since moving to Austin and spend my free time hanging out practicing my photography skills using Austin as my canvas.
